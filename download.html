System Requirements

iPad & iPhone

AUv3
  • iPadOS / iOS 15+
  • Apple A12 chip or newer
  • 100 MB free disk space
  • Internet connection
  • Compatible hosts: AUM, Drambo, Loopy Pro, Cubasis
  • Third-party plugin or hardware to generate drum sounds

Mac

AUv3VST3
  • macOS 11+
  • 4 GB RAM
  • Apple Silicon or Intel 3.4 GHz CPU
  • 100 MB free disk space
  • Internet connection
  • Compatible hosts: Ableton Live, Logic Pro, Cubase, FL Studio, Bitwig, REAPER
  • Third-party plugins or hardware to generate drum sounds

Windows

VST3
  • Windows 10+
  • 4 GB RAM
  • Intel 64-bit, 4 cores, 3.4 GHz CPU
  • 100 MB free disk space
  • Internet connection
  • Compatible hosts: Ableton Live, Cubase, FL Studio, Bitwig, REAPER
  • Third-party plugins or hardware to generate drum sounds
